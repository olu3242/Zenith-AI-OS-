Scoring Model

Each control is scored 0–5: Missing → Ad-hoc → Partial → Functional → Production-grade → Standard-ready. Domain score = average × 20 (0–100 scale).

Weighted Aggregation

Domains are weighted by operational criticality. Security (10%) and Agents (10%) carry the most weight. A zero in Policy Engine alone costs you 9 points overall.

Evidence-Based

Every score is justified by specific evidence: table existence, RLS verification, cross-tenant test results, tool registry inspection. No guesswork.

Repeatable

Run the audit every quarter. Track your score over time. The 90-day roadmap is designed to improve your score by 15–25 points per cycle.
